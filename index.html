What is Cockroach Janta Party?

The Cockroach Janta Party (CJP) is a satirical, meme-driven digital movement born in May 2026. It started as a defiant joke after Chief Justice Surya Kant allegedly compared unemployed youth to "cockroaches". What began as an insult was reclaimed as a badge of honor celebrating resilience, persistence, and the unkillable spirit of India's youth.

Why Cockroach?

Cockroaches are ultimate survivors — 300+ million years, can live without food for a month. Just like India's unemployed youth: underestimated but UNKILLABLE.
